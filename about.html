About Us

Welcome to Addison's Poke – where passion meets perfection in every bowl! At Addison's Poke, we believe that food should be more than just nourishment; it should be an experience. Our journey began with a simple yet profound love for poke—a traditional Hawaiian dish that combines fresh, vibrant ingredients into a harmonious symphony of flavors. What started as a small family kitchen experiment quickly grew into a thriving culinary adventure, thanks to our commitment to quality, authenticity, and innovation.
